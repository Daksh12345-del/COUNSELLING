How Our Counselling Works

A structured 5-step process designed to eliminate confusion and get you to the right college.

01

Initial Meeting

We understand your marks, board, interests, budget, and preferred location — online or offline.

02

College Shortlisting

We map your rank and cut-off to shortlist 15–30 realistic and aspirational college options.

03

Form-Filling Support

Guidance on filling choices, locking/unlocking preferences, and avoiding common mistakes.

04

Deadline Tracking

We monitor official websites, counselling dates, and document submission deadlines for you.

05

Final Admission

We stay with you until your seat is confirmed and all formalities are complete.
